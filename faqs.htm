Frequently Asked Questions...

What is macroBLASTER?

It's a free online tool that allows you to calculate a healthy, daily, macronutrient balance based on your personal attributes and the highly-regarded Harris-Benedict formula.

Are you affiliated with the companies I can find through the website?

No, we are 100% independent. We see ourselves as a tool to encourage others to seek a more active, healthy lifestyle. All our links are provided impartially, it is up to the user to make their selections based on their own assessment of the available information.
